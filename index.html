This is the GOW Gratitude Challenge — 30 days of showing up, posting what you are grateful for, and doing it together as a community rooted in faith.
How it works — 01
Post every day, build your streak
Once a day, write one thing you are grateful for and hit Share. Every day you show up adds to your streak. Miss a day and it resets — so show up.
Milestones at Day 3, 7, 14, 21 and 30
How it works — 02
The community wall
Every approved post goes live on the wall for the whole community to see. Like a post. Say Amen. Leave a comment. This is not social media — it is a family showing up for each other.
How it works — 03
Bring someone with you
Gratitude grows when it is shared. Your profile has a personal invite link — send it to one person who needs this right now. That is how this community grows.

Our mission
GOW exists to raise up a generation anchored in the Word of God — equipped to live by it, not just know it. Every challenge, every piece of content is built around one thing: identity in Christ.
The Gratitude Challenge
Every April, the GOW community commits to 30 days of gratitude. One post a day. One thing you are grateful for. Thirty days of building a habit rooted in faith — together.
