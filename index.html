About GOW
GOW
Generation Of The Word
A generation living by the Word of God
Our mission
GOW exists to raise up a generation that does not just know the Word of God — but lives by it. Every challenge, every garment, every piece of content points back to identity in Christ.
The Gratitude Challenge
Every April, GOW hosts a 30-day online challenge. One post a day. One thing you are grateful for. Thirty days of building a habit rooted in faith and identity.
Founded by Gershom Adeniyi
Founder of GOW and Genesiah. Voice for a generation finding identity in Christ. Based in Edmonton, Alberta.
